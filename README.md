# Circle Downloader — Download Videos from Circle.so Communities, Tella, Loom, and More

SERP Circle Downloader is a browser extension that saves videos from Circle.so communities, Tella.tv recordings, Loom videos, and embedded content from YouTube, Vimeo, and Wistia. Open a lesson or post with a video, click the extension icon, pick your quality, and it saves as MP4. No external software needed. 3 free downloads included.

## Why Circle Downloader

Circle.so has no native download button, Tella recordings expire, and Loom auto-deletes older videos on free plans. Course platforms embed videos from multiple sources with no unified download option. Circle Downloader handles all of them in one extension — detecting videos across 7+ platforms and converting streams to standard MP4 files directly in your browser.

## Key features

- Multi-platform detection — Circle.so, Tella.tv, Loom.com, YouTube, Vimeo, Wistia
- Finds embedded players hidden inside Circle.so web components
- Converts streaming video to downloadable MP4 files in-browser
- Quality selector with all available resolutions per platform
- Up to 3 concurrent downloads with real-time progress, speed, and cancel
- YouTube URL display with one-click yt-dlp copy for Mac and Windows
- Right-click context menu for quick downloads
- Auto-saves to an organized Circle Downloader subfolder in Downloads
- Works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Firefox, Whale, and Yandex

## How it works

1. **Install** — Add SERP Circle Downloader to your browser
2. **Navigate** — Go to a Circle.so lesson, Tella recording, Loom video, or any page with embedded video
3. **Play** — Start the video so the extension can detect the stream
4. **Download** — Click the extension icon, pick your quality, and hit Download — saved as MP4

## FAQ

**What sites does this extension support**
Circle.so (and Circle.com), Tella.tv, Loom.com, YouTube.com, Vimeo.com, and Wistia.com. It also detects generic embedded video players on other sites.

**What quality options are available**
The extension detects all available resolutions from the source platform and lists them in the quality selector, sorted highest to lowest.

**Where are my downloads saved**
Videos automatically save to a Circle Downloader subfolder inside your browser's default Downloads directory.

**Can I download multiple videos at once**
Yes. The extension supports up to 3 concurrent downloads with individual progress tracking.

**Why isn't the extension finding my video**
Press play on the video first. The extension needs the stream to start before detection works. If issues persist, refresh the page and try again.

**Is my data safe**
Yes. All video processing happens entirely in your browser. No video data is sent to external servers.

## Notes (important)

Only download content you **own** or have **explicit permission** to download. This extension is intended for downloading content you have legitimate access to. Respect content creators' terms of service.
